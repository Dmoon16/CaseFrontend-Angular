Sida loo Sameeyo App

Maqaalkani wuxuu tusinayaa isticmaalayaasha cusub sida loo sameeyo akoon tijaabo ah oo bilaash ah. Case Active waxay si otomaatig ah u siisay isticmaalaha cusub mudnaanta maamulka xisaabtan.

Samaynta xisaab

  1. Tag bogga galitaanka
  2. Guji badhanka Bilow
  3. Geli ciwaanka iimaylka
  4. Guji Next.

img

Xaqiijinta akoon

  1. Fur browser cusub oo gal xisaabtaada iimaylka
  2. Nuqul ka samee koodka lixda lambar ah.
  3. Ku soo noqo tab kiska Firfircoon.
  4. Ku dheji koodka gudaha garoonka Daaqad cusub ayaa soo bandhigaysa.

Daaqada cusub:

  1. Buuxi meelaha loo baahan yahay
  2. Akhri shuruudaha adeegga oo calaamadee sanduuqa si aad u heshid.
  3. Guji SAMEY xisaab bilaash ah.

img

Dhamaystirka dejinta abka

Socodka shaqada ayaa ku soo bandhigaya bogga xiga. Buuxi tillaabooyinka si aad u dhammaystirto dejinta abkaaga. Waxaad mar walba ku noqon kartaa boggagan si aad u saxdo ama u cusboonaysiiso macluumaadka ama heerka akoonkaaga.

Bogga ku saabsan App-ka, ku buuxi magaca shirkadda iyo goobaha mareegaha (loo baahan yahay) ka dibna dhagsii Next.

Bogga macluumaadka biilasha, dhagsii goobta Qorshaha Lacag-bixinta oo dib u eeg fursadaha. Dejinta caadiga ah waa bilaash. Guji GUDBI.

img

Kadib markaad gudbiso foomka, Case Active waxa uu soo bandhigayaa bogga Apps-ka. App kasta oo aad abuurto ama ka tirsan tahay, boggu wuxuu muujinayaa:

  1. Magaca abka
  2. Noocaaga (doorka isticmaalaha)
  3. Xaalada

Bogga Apps waa bar bilow u ah hawlo badan oo maamul:

  1. Guji badhanka APP si aad ula xidhiidho, u buuxiso dukumeentiyada, wada sheekaysiga fiidyaha (iyo in ka badan) arrimaha la xidhiidha kiiska.
  2. Guji badhanka ADMIN si aad u furto guddiga maamulka Tani waa halka ay maamulayaashu ku maamulaan isticmaalayaasha, kiisaska, isku dhafka, iyo in ka badan.
  3. Guji ellipsis si aad u maamusho biilasha akoontada iyo dejimaha ogeysiinta.
  • Xusuusin Case Active waxa uu soo bandhigayaa badhanka ADMIN ee isticmaalayaasha maamulka. Isticmaalayaasha aan lahayn mudnaanta maamulka ma arkaan badhanka ADMIN (sida ka muuqata shaashadda hoose).
