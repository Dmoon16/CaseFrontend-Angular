Yadda Admins Ke Ƙirƙirar Masu Amfani

Masu gudanarwa suna ƙirƙira, gyara ko kashe wanda zai iya samun damar aikace-aikace da lokuta. Lokacin da mai amfani ya shiga dandalin a karon farko, suna buƙatar kammala aikin bayanin martaba.

  • Danna nan don ƙarin koyo game da sabon aikin bayanin martabar mai amfani.

Ƙirƙirar mai amfani

Teburin da ke ƙasa yana nuna hanyoyi biyar don ƙirƙirar mai amfani. Danna hanyar haɗi don koyon yadda.

| Hanyar | Nau'in | Ayyuka | Cikakkun bayanai | Sabon mai amfani yana karɓar sanarwa? |
|----------------------- ------------------- -------------------|

Amfani da UI | UI | ƙara & gyara | Maɓallin Admin> Masu amfani | Babu |

Yayin ƙirƙirar harka kuma sanya mai amfani zuwa harka | UI | ƙara | Maɓallin mai gudanarwa > Harkoki > Sabon maɓalli > Ƙirƙiri shafin Case | Ee, idan an kunna |

Ƙirƙirar mai amfani ta atomatik ta amfani da hanyar shiga jama'a | Ayyukan mai amfani | ƙara | Maɓallin Admin > Saituna > Shafin Saita Tsarin | Ee, idan an kunna akwati ta atomatik |

Shigo da kayan aiki | UI | ƙara & gyara | Maɓallin Gudanarwa > Haɗuwa > Shigowa | Babu |

Sanarwa

Dandalin ba ya aika masu amfani da sanarwa lokacin da aka ƙara su zuwa app. Koyaya, admins na iya ba da sanarwar sanarwa don ayyukan shari'a.
