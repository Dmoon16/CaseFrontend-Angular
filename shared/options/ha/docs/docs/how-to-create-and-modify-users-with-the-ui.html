Yadda ake Ƙirƙiri da Gyara Masu amfani tare da UI

Wannan labarin yana nuna admins yadda ake ƙirƙira masu amfani da sabunta bayanai tare da ƙirar mai amfani da dandamali. Admins kuma na iya ƙara masu amfani zuwa sabon harka da kuma gyara abubuwan da ke akwai.

Ƙirƙirar mai amfani

  1. Shiga cikin asusun admin.
  2. Nemo app akan shafin Apps kuma danna maɓallin ADMIN.
  3. A kan panel Admin, danna menu na Masu amfani.
  4. Danna NEW maballin. Wani sabon taga yana nuni.

Gyara mai amfani

Don sabunta ƙaramin adadin bayanan mai amfani, yi amfani da kayan aikin Gyara kuma yi canje-canje ga kowane mai amfani.

  • Bayanan kula Don canza adadi mai yawa na bayanan mai amfani a zama ɗaya, yi la'akari da amfani da kayan aikin shigo da .
  1. A kan panel Admin, danna menu na Masu amfani.
  2. Nemo mai amfani kuma danna Shirya.
  3. Yi canje-canje kuma danna Ajiye.

Matakai na gaba

Case Active baya aika wa mai amfani sanarwar imel. Masu gudanarwa yakamata su tuntuɓi mai amfani kuma su aika musu hanyar haɗi zuwa shafin gida na Case Active. Sannan mai amfani ya danna maɓallin LOGIN kuma ya kammala aikin aiki don sabbin sa hannu.

  • Wannan labarin yana bayyana tsarin yin rajista don sabbin masu amfani.
