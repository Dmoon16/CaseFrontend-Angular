Yadda ake Ƙirƙirar Masu Amfani da hanyar shiga

Wannan labarin yana nuna admins yadda ake ƙirƙirar masu amfani ta hanyar aika hanyar shiga rajista.

Ana saita hanyar haɗin yanar gizo

  1. Danna maɓallin Aika musu hanyar shiga a ƙasan akwatin maganganu na Ƙara mai amfani. Wani sabon shafi yana nuni.
  2. Sanya zaɓuɓɓukan rajista. (duba ƙasa)

Tabbatar da filin Bada izinin shiga don saita zuwa Kunnawa. (an buƙata) Lokacin da aka kunna, Case Active yana haifar da hanyar haɗi ta musamman.

Bincika zaɓuɓɓukan da ke ƙasa sannan danna gunkin don kwafi hanyar haɗin. Kuna iya sanya hanyar haɗin yanar gizon ku ko aika hanyar haɗin zuwa ɗaya ko fiye da mutane ta imel ko kowace tashar sadarwa.

Ƙirƙirar harka

Da zaɓin, zaku iya ƙirƙirar ƙara ta atomatik ga kowane sabon mai amfani kuma sanya rawar mai amfani.

  1. Danna filin Ƙirƙirar Case ta atomatik kuma zaɓi Kunnawa. Filin Matsayin Mai amfani Case yana nuni.
  2. Danna Matsayin Mai Amfani kuma zaɓi wani zaɓi. (duba ƙasa)

Matsayin mai amfani

Akwai matsayin mai amfani guda uku.

  • abokan ciniki
  • tsoho
  • ma'aikata

Aika hanyar haɗin gwiwa

Raba hanyar haɗin gwiwa tare da mutane ɗaya ko fiye. Kowane mutum yana danna hanyar haɗin yanar gizon kuma ya kammala aikin aiki don kunna asusun Case Active. Da zarar mai amfani ya gama aikin sa hannu, Case Active yana ƙara mai amfani zuwa lissafin Masu amfani.

img

Matakai na gaba

Case Active yana aika wa mai amfani sanarwar imel tare da hanyar shiga. Mai amfani yana danna hanyar haɗin yanar gizon kuma ya kammala aikin aiki don sababbin sa hannu.

  • Wannan labarin yana bayyana tsarin yin rajista don sabbin masu amfani.
