Yadda ake Sanya ko Cire Ayyukan Mai amfani tare da Lambobin da suka gabata

Wannan labarin yana nuna admins yadda ake sanyawa da cire masu amfani da harka. Muna kuma nuna muku yadda ake canza bayanan mai amfani.

Bude harka da ke akwai

  1. Shiga cikin asusun admin.
  2. Nemo app akan shafin Apps kuma danna maɓallin ADMIN.
  3. Danna menu na Cases .
  4. Tabbatar an zaɓi ACTIVE a cikin menu na ƙasa.
  5. Gungura ƙasa zuwa ginshiƙin ACTION.

Sanya ko cirewa

Danna Sanya Masu amfani don sanya mai amfani zuwa shari'ar ko cire mai amfani daga shari'ar (amma ba dandamali ba). A shafi na gaba: Danna ADD kuma kammala aikin aiki. Danna cire don share mai amfani.

img

Gyara

Ba zai yiwu a gyara ko sabunta bayanan mai amfani ba akan shafin Cases. Maimakon haka, bi waɗannan matakan:

  1. Danna menu na Masu amfani .
  2. Nemo sunan mai amfani kuma danna Shirya .
  3. Yi canje-canje kuma danna Ajiye.
