Yadda ake Sanya, Gyara ko Cire Ayyukan Mai Amfani tare da Kayan Aikin Shigo

Wannan labarin yana nuna admins yadda ake sarrafa masu amfani da kayan aikin shigo da kaya.

Admins na iya:

  • sanya masu amfani zuwa wani akwati
  • gyara masu amfani da aka riga aka sanya su zuwa harka
  • cire masu amfani da aka sanya wa wani harka

Bude kayan aikin shigo da kaya

  1. Shiga cikin asusun admin.
  2. Nemo app akan shafin Apps kuma danna maɓallin ADMIN.
  3. Danna maballin Haɗin kai sannan kuma SHIGOwa akan menu na ƙasa.
  4. Danna NEW maballin. Akwatin maganganu na Ƙara Imports.

Ƙirƙirar bayanai

Zazzage fayilolin CSV ɗaya ko fiye. Yi amfani da waɗannan fayilolin don ƙara, gyara, ko cire masu amfani da harka. Bayan akwatin maganganu na Ƙara Imports ya buɗe:

  1. Danna filin Nau'in.
  2. Gungura cikin menu mai saukewa kuma zaɓi wani zaɓi (duba ƙasa). Hanya na nuni.
  3. Danna mahaɗin Zazzage Samfurin. Zazzage fayil ɗin CSV zuwa na'urarka.

Buɗe fayil ɗin kuma ƙara bayanai. Danna nan don koyon yadda ake amfani da bayanan tsari a cikin fayil ɗin CSV.

Zaɓuɓɓukan fayil

Sanya masu amfani zuwa lokuta * Zaɓi wannan zaɓi don ƙara masu amfani zuwa harka. * Bar case_role_order cell blank. * Je zuwa shafin Masu amfani kuma kwafi Tag ID na wannan mutumin. Idan filin Tag ID ba komai bane, shigar da ID na musamman, danna Ajiye, sannan kwafi ID ɗin zuwa fayil ɗin CSV.

Gyara masu amfani da aka sanya su zuwa lokuta * Zaɓi wannan zaɓi don sabunta bayanai don masu amfani na yanzu da aka sanya wa harka. * Yi amfani da ID na Case Tag don ginshiƙin case_ID.

Cire masu amfani da aka sanya wa shari'o'i * Zaɓi wannan zaɓi don cire masu amfani da aka sanya wa harka.

Ana loda fayil ɗin

Kuna iya loda fayil nan da nan ko komawa zuwa akwatin maganganu na Ƙara Imports a kwanan wata kuma kammala waɗannan matakan.

  1. Buɗe akwatin maganganu Ƙara Imports.
  2. Shigar da suna na musamman don fayil ɗin CSV.
  3. Danna alamar ƙari (+) don loda fayil ɗin daga na'urarka.
  4. Danna filin Nau'in kuma zaɓi wani zaɓi. (duba sama)
  5. Danna Ajiye.

Gudanarwa

Dangane da adadin bayanan da ke cikin fayil ɗin da nauyin tsarin, dandamali na iya ɗaukar mintuna da yawa don aiwatar da shigo da kaya.

Lokacin da shigo da kaya ya cika, dandamali yana daidaita bayanan mai amfani (watau ƙarawa, gyara, ko cirewa) akan shafin Cases.

Kasawa

Idan aikin shigo da kaya ya gaza, dandamali yana dawo da saƙon da bai yi nasara ba. A mafi yawan lokuta, dalilin gazawar shine tsara bayanai.

Don ganin dalilin gazawar:

  1. Danna Duba ƙarƙashin taken Aiki.
  2. A shafi na gaba, karkatar da linzamin kwamfuta akan gunkin da bai yi nasara ba. Tushen kayan aiki yana nuna kuskuren.
  3. Jeka fayil ɗin CVS kuma daidaita kuma gyara kuskuren.
  4. Saka fayil ɗin kuma.
