Yadda ake Ƙirƙiri App

Wannan labarin yana nuna sabbin masu amfani yadda ake ƙirƙirar asusun gwaji kyauta. Case Active ta atomatik yana ba sabon gata mai gudanarwa na wannan asusun.

Ƙirƙirar lissafi

  1. Jeka shafin shiga .
  2. Danna maɓallin Fara farawa.
  3. Shigar da adireshin imel.
  4. Danna Gaba.

img

Tabbatar da asusun

  1. Bude sabon shafin burauza kuma shiga cikin asusun imel ɗin ku.
  2. Kwafi lambar lambobi shida.
  3. Komawa shafin Case Active.
  4. Manna lambar a cikin filayen. Wani sabon taga yana nuni.

A cikin sabuwar taga:

  1. Cika filayen da ake buƙata.
  2. Karanta sharuɗɗan sabis kuma duba akwatin don yarda.
  3. Danna Ƙirƙiri ACCOUNT KYAUTA.

Ana kammala saitin app

Gudun aiki yana nuni akan shafi na gaba. Cika matakan don gama saita ƙa'idar ku. Kuna iya komawa zuwa waɗannan shafuka koyaushe don gyara ko sabunta bayanai ko matsayin asusun ku.

A kan Game da App shafi, cika sunan kamfani da filayen gidan yanar gizon (an buƙata) sannan danna Next.

img

A shafin Bayanin Kuɗi, danna filin Shirin Biyan kuɗi kuma duba zaɓuɓɓukan. Saitin tsoho kyauta ne. Danna SAURI.

img

Bayan kun ƙaddamar da fom ɗin, Case Active yana nuna shafin Apps. Ga kowace ƙa'ida da kuka ƙirƙira ko mallakarta, shafin yana nuna:

  1. Sunan app
  2. Nau'in ku (rawar mai amfani)
  3. Matsayi

Shafin Apps shine wurin ƙaddamarwa don ayyukan gudanarwa da yawa:

  1. Danna maɓallin APP don sadarwa, cika takardu, taɗi na bidiyo (da ƙari) kan batutuwan da suka shafi harka.
  2. Danna maballin ADMIN don buɗe admin panel. Wannan shine inda Admins ke sarrafa masu amfani, lokuta, haɗin kai, da ƙari.
  3. Danna ellipsis don sarrafa lissafin lissafin asusu da saitunan sanarwa.
  • Bayanin Case Active yana nuna maɓallin ADMIN don masu amfani da gudanarwa. Masu amfani waɗanda ba su da gata na admin ba sa ganin maɓallin ADMIN (kamar yadda aka nuna a allon ƙasa).
