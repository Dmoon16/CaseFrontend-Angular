Yuav ua li cas Admins tsim cov neeg siv

Admins tsim, hloov kho lossis lov tes taw uas tuaj yeem nkag mus rau cov apps thiab rooj plaub. Thaum tus neeg siv nkag mus rau hauv lub platform thawj zaug, lawv yuav tsum ua kom tiav qhov profile workflow.

  • Nyem qhov no kom paub ntau ntxiv txog tus neeg siv tshiab profile workflow.

Kev ceeb toom

Lub platform tsis xa cov neeg siv kev ceeb toom thaum lawv ntxiv rau ib qho app. Txawm li cas los xij, cov thawj coj tuaj yeem ua kom muaj kev ceeb toom rau cov xwm txheej.
