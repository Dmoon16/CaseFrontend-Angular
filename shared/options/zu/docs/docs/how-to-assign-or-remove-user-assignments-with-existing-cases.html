Ungawabela Kanjani Noma Ususe Izabelo Zomsebenzisi Ezimweni Ezikhona

Lesi sihloko sibonisa abaphathi ukuthi babanikeza kanjani futhi basuse abasebenzisi becala. Siphinde sikubonise ukuthi ungayishintsha kanjani idatha yomsebenzisi.

Ukuvula ikesi elikhona

  1. Ngena ngemvume ku-akhawunti yomqondisi.
  2. Thola uhlelo lokusebenza ekhasini lezinhlelo zokusebenza bese uchofoza inkinobho ethi ADMIN.
  3. Chofoza ithebhu yemenyu ye- Cases .
  4. Qinisekisa ukuthi okuthi ACTIVE kukhethiwe kumenyu encane.
  5. Skrolela phansi kukholomu ACTION.

Yabela noma susa

Chofoza okuthi Yabela Abasebenzisi ukuze unikeze umsebenzisi icala noma ususe umsebenzisi kukesi (kodwa hhayi inkundla). Ekhasini elilandelayo: Chofoza okuthi ADD bese uqedela ukuhamba komsebenzi. Chofoza okuthi susa ukuze ususe umsebenzisi.

Lungisa

Akwenzeki ukuhlela noma ukubuyekeza idatha yomsebenzisi ekhasini lezimo. Kunalokho, landela lezi zinyathelo:

  1. Chofoza ithebhu yemenyu Abasebenzisi .
  2. Thola igama lomsebenzisi bese uchofoza okuthi Hlela .
  3. Yenza izinguquko bese uchofoza LINDISA.
