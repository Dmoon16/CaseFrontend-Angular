Ubanikeza Kanjani Abasebenzisi Ngokuzenzakalelayo Ngesixhumanisi Sokubhaliswa Komphakathi

Abalawuli bafunda ukulungisa izilungiselelo zesistimu ukuze abasebenzisi abasha babelwe ngokuzenzakalelayo kukesi elisanda kwakhiwa ngokuzenzakalela.

Ivula ikhasi lokusetha

Isinyathelo sokuqala ukuvula ikhasi le-Systems Setup.

  1. Ngena ngemvume ku-akhawunti yomqondisi.
  2. Thola uhlelo lokusebenza ekhasini lezinhlelo zokusebenza bese uchofoza inkinobho ethi ADMIN.
  3. Chofoza ithebhu ethi Amasistimu bese UKUSETHA UHLELO kumenyu encane.
  4. Skrolela phansi ku-USER SIGNUP isihloko.

Ilungiselela izilungiselelo zesistimu

  1. Setha amamenyu okudonsela phansi okuthi Vumela Ukubhalisa futhi Udale Ngokuzenzakalelayo Ikesi ukuthi Uvule.
  2. Chofoza imenyu yendima bese ukhetha inketho (okuzenzakalelayo kuyanconywa).
  3. Kopisha futhi wabelane ngesixhumanisi Sokubhalisa Umsebenzisi Womphakathi.

Ithumela isixhumanisi

Umsebenzisi ngamunye ochofoza isixhumanisi futhi aqedele ukubhalisa ucupha inkundla ukuthi ikhiqize isimo esisha ekhasini Lamacala futhi inikeze ngokuzenzakalelayo umsebenzisi kuleso simo.
