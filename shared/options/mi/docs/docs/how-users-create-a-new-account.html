Me pehea nga Kaiwhakamahi Waihanga Kaute Hou

Whai muri i te taapiri a te kaiwhakahaere i tetahi kaiwhakamahi ki tetahi keehi: * mena he hou te kaiwhakamahi ki te keehi, me whakaoti e te kaiwhakamahi he rerengamahi ki te hanga i tetahi kaute me te whakaae ki te uru atu ki te keehi. * Mēnā he kaute pūmau te kaiwhakamahi, me whakaae te kaiwhakamahi ki te tono kia uru atu ki te keehi.

Ko tenei tuhinga e whakaatu ana i nga waahanga me whakaoti e te kaiwhakamahi ki te hanga i tana kaute.

Te timatanga

E rua nga huarahi ka taea te taapiri i tetahi kaiwhakamahi ki tetahi taupānga kaha

  • Na te kaiwhakahaere: Mena ka hangaia e te kaiwhakahaere he kaiwhakamahi ma te papaaho UI , taputapu Kawemai ranei , me tuku te kaiwhakahaere ki te kaiwhakamahi he hono ki account.caseactive.com kia haere tonu ki te takiuru.
  • Na te kaiwhakamahi: Mena i hanga e te kaiwhakamahi he kaute ma te whakamahi i te hono hainatanga , ka tukuna aunoatia te kaiwhakamahi ki account.caseactive.com ki te takiuru.

Te takiuru

Ina takiuru te kaiwhakamahi ki tana putea CaseActive mo te wa tuatahi, ka akiaki te papaaho ki te kaiwhakamahi ki:

  • whakatuwheratia ta ratou kaute imeera ka kape / whakapiri i te waehere haumaru e ono-mati
  • whakaotihia he kōtaha pūkete

img

Whai muri i te paato a te kaiwhakamahi ki te WHAKAARO, ka whakaatu te wharangi Taupānga. Ko te wharangi e whakaatu ana i te ingoa o te taupānga (ingoa kamupene), te momo kaiwhakamahi (turanga), te mana, me te paatene POIA.

img

Hei uru atu ki te taupānga, me whakaae te kaiwhakamahi ki te powhiri. Me:

  1. Patohia te paatene POIA.
  2. Arotakehia nga whakaaetanga.
  3. Patohia te ripa Whakamōhiotanga hei arotake me te whakatika (he kōwhiri) ngā kupu.
  4. Patohia te WHAKAARO ACCESS.

img

Ina whakaae te kaiwhakamahi, ka whakaatuhia e te papaahi he paatene APP ki te wharangi kaiwhakamahi. Ma tenei paatene ka uru te kaiwhakamahi ki nga keehi kua tohua.

Hei whakatika i nga tautuhinga kaiwhakamahi, ka pao te kaiwhakamahi i te ellipsis.

img

Ka taea e te kaiwhakamahi: * te tango i te uru ki te taupānga * te whakarereke i nga tautuhinga panui

img

I muri i te whakaotinga a te kaiwhakamahi i te rerengamahi, ka puta te ingoa kaiwhakamahi ki te rarangi Kaiwhakamahi a te kaiwhakahaere. Ko te rarangi e whakaatu ana i nga kaiwhakamahi katoa, kua takiuru, kua kore ranei.
