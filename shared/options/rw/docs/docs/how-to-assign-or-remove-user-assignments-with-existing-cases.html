Uburyo bwo Kugena cyangwa Gukuraho Umukoresha Umukoro hamwe Imanza Zihari

Iyi ngingo yerekana abadmin uburyo bwo kugena no gukuraho abakoresha imanza. Turakwereka kandi uburyo bwo guhindura amakuru yumukoresha.

Gufungura urubanza ruriho

  1. Injira kuri konte ya admin.
  2. Shakisha porogaramu kurupapuro rwa porogaramu hanyuma ukande buto ya ADMIN.
  3. Kanda ahanditse Imanza .
  4. Menya neza ko IGIKORWA cyatoranijwe muri submenu.
  5. Kanda hasi kumurongo wibikorwa.

img

Shinga cyangwa ukureho

Kanda Kugenera Abakoresha kugirango bagenere umukoresha murubanza cyangwa ukure umukoresha murubanza (ariko ntabwo ari urubuga). Kurupapuro rukurikira: Kanda ADD hanyuma urangize akazi. Kanda gukuramo kugirango usibe umukoresha.

img

Hindura

Ntabwo bishoboka guhindura cyangwa kuvugurura amakuru yumukoresha kurupapuro. Ahubwo, kurikiza izi ntambwe:

  1. Kanda ahanditse Abakoresha .
  2. Shakisha izina ryumukoresha hanyuma ukande ahanditse .
  3. Kora impinduka hanyuma ukande SAVE.
