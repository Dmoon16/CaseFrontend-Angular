Abasebenzisi Bayenza njani iAkhawunti eNtsha

Emva kokuba umlawuli esongeza umsebenzisi kwityala: * Ukuba umsebenzisi umtsha kwi-caseactive, umsebenzisi kufuneka agqibezele ukuqhutywa komsebenzi ukwenza i-akhawunti kwaye amkele isimemo sokufikelela kwityala. * ukuba umsebenzisi une-caseactive acounnt ekhoyo, umsebenzisi kufuneka amkele isimemo sokufikelela kwimeko.

Eli nqaku lichaza amanyathelo ekufuneka umsebenzisi awagqibe ukwenza iakhawunti yakhe.

Ndiyaqalisa

Kukho iindlela ezimbini umsebenzisi onokongezwa kwi-caseactive app

  • Ngomlawuli: Ukuba umlawuli udala umsebenzisi ngeqonga le-UI okanye isixhobo sokungeniswa ngaphandle , umlawuli kufuneka athumele umsebenzisi ikhonkco account.caseactive.com ukuze uqhubeke nokungena.
  • Ngomsebenzisi: Ukuba umsebenzisi wenze iakhawunti esebenzisa ikhonkco lokubhalisa , umsebenzisi uya kuthunyelwa ngokuzenzekelayo kwi-account.caseactive.com ukuze uqhubeke nokungena.

Ngena

Xa umsebenzisi engena kwiakhawunti yakhe ye-CaseActive okokuqala, iqonga likhuthaza umsebenzisi ukuba:

  • bavule iakhawunti yabo ye-imeyile kwaye emva koko ukope/uncamathisele ikhowudi yokhuseleko enamanani amathandathu
  • gqibezela iprofayile yeakhawunti

img

Emva kokuba umsebenzisi ecofa UPDATE, iphepha leApps liyaboniswa. Iphepha libonisa igama le-app (igama lenkampani), uhlobo lomsebenzisi (indima), ubume, kunye neqhosha elithi ISIMEMO.

Ukufikelela kwi-app, umsebenzisi kufuneka amkele isimemo. Umsebenzisi kufuneka:

  1. Cofa iqhosha elithi ISIMEMO.
  2. Hlaziya iimvume.
  3. Cofa i Izaziso tab ukujonga kwaye uhlele (ukhetho) imigaqo.
  4. Cofa UVUMELEKO LOKUFIKELELA.

Xa umsebenzisi evuma, iqonga libonisa iqhosha le-APP kwiphepha lomsebenzisi. Eli qhosha linika unikezelo lomsebenzisi kwiimeko ezabelweyo.

Ukuhlela useto lomsebenzisi, umsebenzisi ucofa i-ellipsis.
