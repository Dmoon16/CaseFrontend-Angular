Indlela abalawuli ababenza ngayo abasebenzisi

Abalawuli benza, balungise okanye bavale ukuba ngubani onokufikelela kwii-apps kunye namatyala. Xa umsebenzisi engena eqongeni okokuqala, kufuneka agqibezele iprofayile.

  • Cofa apha ukuze ufunde ngakumbi malunga nomsebenzi omtsha weprofayile yomsebenzisi.

Ukudala umsebenzisi

Itheyibhile engezantsi ibonisa iindlela ezintlanu zokwenza umsebenzisi. Cofa ikhonkco ukufunda ukuba njani.

| Indlela | Uhlobo | Iintshukumo | Iinkcukacha | Umsebenzisi omtsha ufumana isaziso? |
|------------------------------------------------- ------|------------------------------------------ -------------------------|

Ukusebenzisa i-UI | UI | yongeza kwaye ulungise | Iqhosha lokulawula > Abasebenzisi | Hayi |

Ngelixa usenza ityala kwaye unikeze umsebenzisi kwimeko | UI | yongeza | Iqhosha lolawulo> Iimeko> Iqhosha elitsha> Yenza iphepha letyala | Ewe, ukuba yenziwe yasebenza |

Yenza ngokuzenzekelayo umsebenzisi usebenzisa ikhonkco lokubhalisa esidlangalaleni | Intshukumo yomsebenzisi | yongeza | Iqhosha lokulawula > Useto > Iphepha lokuSeta inkqubo | Ewe, ukuba i-auto create case ivuliwe |

Thatha ngaphandle isixhobo | UI | yongeza kwaye ulungise | Iqhosha lokulawula > Udibaniso > Ukuthunyelwa ngaphandle | Hayi |

Ukusebenzisa i-API (iza kungekudala) | API | yongeza kwaye ulungise | na | na |

Izaziso

Iqonga alithumeli abasebenzisi isaziso xa bongezwa kwi-app. Nangona kunjalo, ii-admins zingenza ukuba izaziso zikwazi ukwenza izabelo zamatyala.
