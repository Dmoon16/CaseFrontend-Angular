Mokhoa oa ho abela kapa ho tlosa likabelo tsa basebelisi ka linyeoe tse teng

Sengoliloeng sena se bonts'a li-admins mokhoa oa ho abela le ho tlosa basebelisi ba linyeoe. Re boetse re u bont≈°a mokhoa oa ho fetola data ea mosebelisi.

Ho bula nyeoe e teng

  1. Kena ho akhaonto ea admin.
  2. Fumana sesebelisoa leqepheng la Lisebelisoa ebe u tobetsa konopo ea ADMIN.
  3. Tobetsa tabeng ea Cases menu.
  4. Etsa bonnete ba hore ACTIVE e khethiloe ho submenu.
  5. Tsamaisetsa tlase ho kholomo ea ACTION.

Abela kapa tlosa

Tobetsa Abela Basebelisi ho abela mosebelisi nyeoeng kapa ho tlosa mosebelisi nyeoeng (empa eseng sethala). Leqepheng le latelang: Tobetsa ADD 'me u phethe mosebetsi. Tobetsa tlosa ho hlakola mosebelisi.

Fetola

Ha ho khonehe ho hlophisa kapa ho ntlafatsa lintlha tsa mosebelisi leqepheng la Maemo. Ho e-na le hoo, latela mehato ena:

  1. Tobetsa tab ya menu ya Users .
  2. Fumana lebitso la mosebelisi ebe o tobetsa Edita .
  3. Etsa liphetoho ebe o tobetsa SAVE.
