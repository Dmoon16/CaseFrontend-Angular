Mokhoa oa ho Abela Basebelisi ka Sehokelo sa Phatlalatso sa Sechaba

Batsamaisi ba ithuta ho hlophisa litlhophiso tsa sistimi e le hore basebelisi ba bacha ba abeloe ka bo bona nyeoeng e sa tsoa etsoa ka boits'oaro.

Ho bula leqephe la ho seta

Mohato oa pele ke ho bula leqephe la Systems Setup.

  1. Kena ho akhaonto ea admin.
  2. Fumana sesebelisoa leqepheng la Lisebelisoa ebe u tobetsa konopo ea ADMIN.
  3. Tobetsa tab ya Litsamaiso ebe SYSTEM SETUP ho submenu.
  4. Tsamaisetsa tlase ho USER SIGNUP sehlooho.

Ho lokisa litlhophiso tsa sistimi

  1. Beha li-menu tse theohang tsa Lumella ho Signup le Auto Create Case ho Bulela.
  2. Tobetsa lethathamo la karolo ebe u khetha khetho (ho khothaletsoa kamehla).
  3. Kopitsa 'me u arolelane sehokelo sa Public User Signup.

Ho romella sehokelo

Mosebelisi e mong le e mong ea tobetsang sehokelo le ho phethela ho ingolisa o etsa hore sethala se hlahise nyeoe e ncha leqepheng la Cases mme se abela mosebelisi boemong boo.
