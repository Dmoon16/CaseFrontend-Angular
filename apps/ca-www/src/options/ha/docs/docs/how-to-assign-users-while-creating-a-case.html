Yadda Ake Sanya Masu Amfani Yayin Ƙirƙirar Harka

Wannan labarin yana nuna admins yadda ake sanya mai amfani yayin ƙirƙirar sabon harka. Idan mai amfani bai riga ya yi rajista akan dandamali ba, zaku iya ƙirƙirar sabon mai amfani yayin ƙirƙirar sabon harka.

Ƙirƙirar harka

  1. Shiga cikin asusun admin.
  2. Nemo app akan shafin Apps kuma danna maɓallin ADMIN.
  3. Danna menu na Cases.
  4. Danna NEW maballin. Wani sabon taga yana nuni.
  • Cika filaye don ƙirƙirar harka sannan gungura ƙasa zuwa jigon masu amfani da aka sanyawa.

Sanya mai amfani

Mai amfani na yanzu

Bi waɗannan matakan don sanya mai amfani na yanzu ga sabon harka.

Danna maɓallin ASSIGN USERS kuma cika filayen.

  1. Danna saukar da mai amfani kuma zaɓi mai ganowa (misali imel).
  2. Shigar da ƙima a cikin fanko (misali adireshin imel).
  3. Idan rikodin mai amfani ya kasance a cikin dandamali, filin yana ba da shawarar suna ta atomatik.
  4. Zaɓi madaidaicin mai amfani.
  5. Zaɓi rawar mai amfani (abokan ciniki, tsoho, ko ma'aikata).
  6. Zabi, duba akwatin don aika mai amfani da sanarwar imel.
  7. Danna Ajiye.

Case Active yana ƙara mai amfani ga harka. Yanzu admins na iya danna Ajiye don rufe taga, ko ƙara sabon mai amfani, ko cire mai amfani.

Ƙara sabon mai amfani

Idan dandamali ba zai iya samun rikodin na yanzu ba, danna maɓallin don ƙara sabon mai amfani.

Cika filayen sannan danna SAVE don ƙara mai amfani zuwa dandamali. Na gaba, saita rawar mai amfani kuma danna sake Ajiye don sanya mai amfani ga harka. A ƙarshe, danna Ajiye ƙarin lokaci don ajiye karar.
