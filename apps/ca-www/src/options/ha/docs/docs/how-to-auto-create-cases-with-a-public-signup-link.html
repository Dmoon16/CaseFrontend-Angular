Yadda ake Ƙirƙirar Cases ta atomatik tare da hanyar haɗin gwiwar Jama'a

Wannan labarin yana nuna admins yadda ake ƙirƙirar harka (kuma ƙara mai amfani a lokaci guda) ta hanyar aika hanyar shiga ɗaya.

Gano wurin mahaɗin

  1. Shiga cikin asusun admin.
  2. Nemo app akan shafin Apps kuma danna maɓallin ADMIN.
  3. A kan panel Admin, danna menu na Saituna.
  4. Tabbatar cewa an zaɓi shafin SYSTEM SETUP.

img

Ana saita saituna

Gungura ƙasa zuwa kan USER SIGNUP.

Tabbatar an saita filin Bada Sa hannu zuwa Kunnawa. (an buƙata) Lokacin da aka kunna wannan fasalin, Case Active yana haifar da hanyar haɗi ta musamman.

  1. Danna filin Ƙirƙirar Case ta atomatik kuma zaɓi Kunnawa. Filin Matsayin Mai Amfani yana nuni.
  2. Zaɓi rawar. (duba ƙasa)

img

Matsayin mai amfani

Akwai matsayin mai amfani guda uku.

  • abokan ciniki (an ba da shawarar)
  • tsoho
  • ma'aikata

Aika hanyar haɗin gwiwa

Raba hanyar haɗin gwiwa tare da mutane ɗaya ko fiye. Kowane mai amfani da ya danna hanyar haɗin kuma ya kammala rajista yana haifar da dandamali don haifar da sabon shari'ar akan shafi kuma sanya mai amfani ta atomatik a cikin harka.
