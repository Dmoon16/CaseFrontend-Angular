Yadda ake Sanya Masu amfani ta atomatik tare da hanyar haɗin gwiwar Jama'a

Admins suna koyon yadda ake saita saitunan tsarin don sabbin masu amfani za a sanya su ta atomatik zuwa sabuwar akwati da aka ƙirƙira ta atomatik.

Bude shafin saitin

Mataki na farko shine buɗe shafin Saitin Tsarin.

  1. Shiga cikin asusun admin.
  2. Nemo app akan shafin Apps kuma danna maɓallin ADMIN.
  3. Danna shafin Systems sannan sannan SYSTEM SETUP akan menu na kasa.
  4. Gungura ƙasa zuwa kan USER SIGNUP.

Yana daidaita saitunan tsarin

  1. Saita Izinin Shiga da Ƙirƙirar Case ta atomatik menus na ƙasa zuwa Kunnawa.
  2. Danna menu na rawar kuma zaɓi wani zaɓi (an bada shawarar tsoho).
  3. Kwafi kuma raba hanyar haɗin gwiwar Sa hannun Mai amfanin Jama'a.

Aika hanyar haɗin gwiwa

Kowane mai amfani da ya danna hanyar haɗin yanar gizon kuma ya kammala rajista yana haifar da dandamali don samar da sabon shari'ar akan shafi na Cases kuma ya sanya mai amfani ta atomatik a wannan yanayin.
