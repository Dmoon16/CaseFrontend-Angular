Yadda ake sabunta bayanan mai amfani

Wannan labarin yana nuna masu amfani yadda ake sabunta bayanai akan shafin Fayil ɗin su.

  • Masu amfani za su iya sabunta bayanan martaba na kansu.
  • Admins na iya sabunta shafin Fayil na kowane mai amfani.

Ga masu amfani

Mataki na farko shine bude shafin Bayanan martaba. Masu amfani da admins suna bin waɗannan matakan don sabunta shafin Bayanan martaba na kansu.

  1. Shiga cikin asusun ku na Case Active.
  2. Danna Profile shafin a saman shafin.
  3. Yi canje-canje.
  4. Danna Ajiye.

Yawancin filayen zaɓi ne, kuma ana buƙatar wasu filayen. Filayen da ake buƙata sune:

  • Sunan rana
  • Sunan mahaifa
  • Adireshin i-mel
  • Harshe
  • Yankin lokaci

Domin admins

Admins kuma na iya sabunta bayanan martaba ga sauran masu amfani. Wannan ya haɗa da filaye guda uku a ƙarƙashin taken HOST USER INFO.

  1. Shiga cikin asusun gudanarwa na Case Active.
  2. Nemo app kuma danna maɓallin ADMIN.
  3. Danna Masu amfani a saman menu.
  4. Je zuwa ƙaramin menu kuma zaɓi jerin masu amfani da kuke son samun dama (ACTIVE ko RASHE). Saitin tsoho yana aiki.
  5. Nemo mai amfani a lissafin kuma danna Shirya.
  6. Yi canje-canje kamar yadda ake buƙata. (duba ƙasa)
  7. Danna Ajiye.

Canja bayanin mai amfani

Admins na iya canza bayanin mai amfani.

  1. Saita Nau'in Mai Amfani (User or Manager).
  2. Saita Matsayin Mai Amfani (Aiki don Rage).
  3. Shigar da takamaiman Tag ID. (na zaɓi)
  4. Danna Ajiye.
