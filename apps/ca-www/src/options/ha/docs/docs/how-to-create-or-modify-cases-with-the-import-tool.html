Yadda ake Ƙirƙiri ko Gyara Harkoki tare da Kayan Aikin Shigo

Wannan labarin yana nuna admins yadda ake ƙirƙira ko gyara lamura masu wanzuwa tare da kayan aikin shigo da kaya.

Bude kayan aikin shigo da kaya

  1. Shiga cikin asusun admin.
  2. Nemo app akan shafin Apps kuma danna maɓallin ADMIN.
  3. A kan panel Admin, danna maɓallin Haɗin kai sannan kuma SHIGOwa akan menu na ƙasa.
  4. Danna NEW maballin. Akwatin maganganu na Ƙara Imports a cikin sabon shafin burauza.

img

Ƙirƙirar lokuta

Bayan akwatin maganganu na Ƙara Imports ya buɗe:

  1. Danna filin Nau'in kuma zaɓi wani zaɓi (duba ƙasa). Hanya na nuni.
  2. Danna mahaɗin Zazzage Samfurin. Zazzage fayil ɗin CSV zuwa na'urarka.

Na gaba, buɗe fayil ɗin kuma ƙara lokuta.

Nau'in

Lokacin da ka danna filin Type , menu mai saukewa yana nuna. Zaɓi ɗaya daga cikin zaɓuɓɓuka biyu don shigo da harka mai girma.

  • Lura Menu mai saukewa yana da zaɓuɓɓuka da yawa, amma biyu kawai suna da alaƙa da sarrafa harka. Kowane zaɓi yana da samfurin CSV daban-daban.

Ƙara lokuta

  • Zaɓi wannan zaɓin don ƙararraki mai sauƙi.
  • Danna mahaɗin Zazzagewar Samfura don buɗe fayil ɗin CSV tare da kanun shafi don Ƙirƙirar nau'in shigo da lokuta.

Gyara lokuta

  • Zaɓi wannan zaɓi don sabunta lamuran yanzu.
  • Danna mahaɗin Zazzage Samfuran don buɗe fayil ɗin CSV tare da kanun shafi don Canza nau'in shigo da lokuta.

Ana loda fayil ɗin

Kuna iya loda fayil nan da nan ko komawa zuwa akwatin maganganu na Ƙara Imports a kwanan wata kuma kammala waɗannan matakan.

  1. Buɗe akwatin maganganu Ƙara Imports.
  2. Shigar da suna na musamman don fayil ɗin CSV.
  3. Danna alamar ƙari (+) don loda fayil ɗin daga na'urarka.
  4. Danna filin Nau'in kuma zaɓi wani zaɓi. (duba sama)
  5. Danna Ajiye.

Tsarin tsari

Waɗannan su ne filayen da ake buƙata da buƙatun farko. Danna nan don duba cikakkun bayanan tsarawa.

tag_id (ko case_ID) mai ganowa na musamman (amfani da haruffa haruffa, ana karɓar saƙa)

case_status 0 = rufe 1 = aiki

date_bude wata/rana/shekara mm/dd/yyyy

game da keɓantaccen bayanin shari'ar (amfani da haruffa haruffa, ana karɓar saƙo)

img

Gudanarwa

Dangane da adadin bayanan da ke cikin fayil ɗin da nauyin tsarin, dandamali na iya ɗaukar mintuna da yawa don aiwatar da shigo da kaya.

img

Lokacin da shigo da kaya ya cika, dandamali yana daidaita bayanan shari'a (watau ƙara ko gyara) akan dashboard na Cases na admin.

Kasawa

Idan aikin shigo da kaya ya gaza, dandamali yana dawo da saƙon da bai yi nasara ba. A mafi yawan lokuta, dalilin gazawar shine tsara bayanai.

img

Don ganin dalilin gazawar:

  1. Danna Duba ƙarƙashin taken ACTION.
  2. A shafi na gaba, karkatar da linzamin kwamfuta akan gunkin da bai yi nasara ba. Tushen kayan aiki yana nuna kuskuren.
  3. Jeka fayil ɗin CVS kuma daidaita kuma gyara kuskuren.
  4. Saka fayil ɗin kuma.
