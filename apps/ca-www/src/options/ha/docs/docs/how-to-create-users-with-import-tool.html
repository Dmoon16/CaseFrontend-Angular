Yadda ake Ƙirƙirar Masu Amfani da Kayan Aikin Shigo

Wannan labarin yana nuna admins yadda ake ƙirƙirar masu amfani tare da kayan aikin shigo da kaya.

Bude kayan aikin shigo da kaya

Masu gudanarwa na iya amfani da kayan aikin shigo da kaya don loda sabbin masu amfani da yawa da kuma canza bayanan mai amfani da ke akwai.

  1. Shiga cikin asusun admin.
  2. Nemo app akan shafin Apps kuma danna maɓallin ADMIN.
  3. A kan panel Admin, danna maɓallin Haɗin kai sannan kuma SHIGOwa akan menu na ƙasa.
  4. Danna NEW maballin. Akwatin maganganu na Ƙara Imports a cikin sabon shafin burauza.

Ƙirƙirar bayanai

Bayan akwatin maganganu na Ƙara Imports ya buɗe:

  1. Danna filin Nau'in kuma zaɓi wani zaɓi (duba ƙasa). Hanya na nuni.
  2. Danna mahaɗin Zazzage Samfurin . Zazzage fayil ɗin CSV zuwa na'urarka.

Na gaba, buɗe fayil ɗin kuma ƙara rikodin. Danna nan don koyon yadda ake amfani da bayanan tsari a cikin fayil ɗin CSV.

Nau'in

Lokacin da ka danna filin Type, menu mai saukewa yana nuna. Zaɓi ɗayan zaɓuɓɓuka biyu don shigo da kaya mai yawa.

:exclamation: NOTE Menu mai saukewa yana da zaɓuɓɓuka da yawa, amma guda biyu ne kawai ke da alaƙa da kayan aikin shigo da kaya. Kowane zaɓi yana da samfurin CSV daban-daban.

Ƙara masu amfani

  • Zaɓi wannan zaɓin don sauƙin lodawa mai yawa na bayanan mai amfani.
  • Danna mahaɗin Zazzage Samfuran don buɗe fayil ɗin CSV tare da kanun shafi don Ƙara nau'in shigo da masu amfani.

Gyara masu amfani

  • Zaɓi wannan zaɓi don sabunta bayanai don masu amfani na yanzu.
  • Danna mahaɗin Zazzage Samfuran don buɗe fayil ɗin CSV tare da kanun shafi don Canza nau'in shigo da masu amfani.

Ana loda fayil ɗin

Kuna iya loda fayil nan da nan ko komawa zuwa akwatin maganganu na Ƙara Imports a kwanan wata kuma kammala waɗannan matakan.

  1. Buɗe akwatin maganganu Ƙara Imports.
  2. Shigar da suna na musamman don fayil ɗin CSV.
  3. Danna alamar ƙari (+) don loda fayil ɗin daga na'urarka.
  4. Danna filin Nau'in kuma zaɓi wani zaɓi. (duba sama)
  5. Danna Ajiye.

Gudanarwa

Dangane da adadin bayanan da ke cikin fayil ɗin da nauyin tsarin, dandamali na iya ɗaukar mintuna da yawa don aiwatar da shigo da kaya.

Lokacin da shigo da kaya ya cika, dandamali yana daidaita bayanan mai amfani (watau ƙara, gyara, ko kashewa) akan dashboard ɗin Masu amfani na mai gudanarwa.

Kasawa

Idan aikin shigo da kaya ya gaza, dandamali yana dawo da saƙon da bai yi nasara ba. A mafi yawan lokuta, dalilin gazawar shine tsara bayanai.

Don ganin dalilin gazawar:

  1. Danna Duba ƙarƙashin taken Aiki.
  2. A shafi na gaba, karkatar da linzamin kwamfuta akan gunkin da bai yi nasara ba. Tushen kayan aiki yana nuna kuskuren.
  3. Jeka fayil ɗin CVS kuma daidaita kuma gyara kuskuren.
  4. Saka fayil ɗin kuma.
