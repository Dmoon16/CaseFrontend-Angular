Sida ay maamulayaashu u abuuraan isticmaalayaasha

Maamulayaashu waxay abuuraan, wax ka beddelaan ama joojiyaan cidda geli karta abka iyo kiisaska. Marka isticmaaluhu uu galo goobta markii ugu horeysay, waxay u baahan yihiin inay dhamaystiraan socodka shaqada profile.

  • Riix halkan si aad wax badan uga ogaato socodka shaqada astaanta isticmaalaha cusub.

Abuuritaanka isticmaale

Jadwalka hoose wuxuu muujinayaa shan siyaabood oo loo abuuro isticmaale. Guji xiriirka si aad u barato sida.

| Habka | Nooca | Ficilada | Faahfaahin | Isticmaale cusub ayaa helaya wargelin? |
|---------------------------- ---------------- -------------|

Isticmaalka UI | UI | ku dar & wax ka beddel | Badhanka maamulka > Isticmaalayaasha | Maya |

Markaad abuureyso kiis oo ku meelayso isticmaale kiis | UI | ku dar | Badhanka maamulka> Kiisaska> badhanka cusub> Abuur bogga kiiska | Haa, haddii la awoodo |

Si otomaatig ah u samee isticmaale adiga oo isticmaalaya isku xirka isdiiwaangelinta dadweynaha | Ficilka isticmaalaha | ku dar | Badhanka maamulka > Settings > System Dejinta bogga | Haa, haddii kiis si otomaatig ah loo abuuro |

Qalabka soo dejinta | UI | ku dar & wax ka beddel | Badhanka maamulka> Isku-dhafka> Soo dejinta | Maya |

Isticmaalka API (oo dhawaan imanaya) | API | ku dar & wax ka beddel | na | na |

Ogeysiisyada

Madalku uma soo diro isticmaalayaasha ogeysiis marka lagu daro abka. Si kastaba ha ahaatee, maamulayaashu waxay awood u siin karaan ogeysiisyada hawl-gudbinta kiiska.
