Me pehea te Tautapa i nga Kaiwhakamahi i te wa e hanga ana he keehi

Ko tenei tuhinga e whakaatu ana ki nga kaiwhakahaere me pehea te tautapa i tetahi kaiwhakamahi i te wa e hanga ana he keehi hou. Mena kaore ano kia rehitatia te kaiwhakamahi i runga i te papanga, ka taea e koe te hanga taapiri i tetahi kaiwhakamahi hou i te wa e hanga ana he keehi hou.

Te hanga take

  1. Takiuru ki te pūkete kaiwhakahaere.
  2. Kimihia he taupānga ki te wharangi Taupānga ka paato i te paatene ADMIN.
  3. Patohia te ripa tahua Take.
  4. Patohia te paatene HOU. Ka whakaatuhia he matapihi hou.
  • Whakakiia nga mara hei hanga keehi katahi ka panuku ki raro ki te upoko Kaiwhakamahi Kua Tautapahia.

Tautapa i te kaiwhakamahi

Kaiwhakamahi o naianei

A pee i enei mahi ki te tautapa i tetahi kaiwhakamahi o naianei ki te keehi hou.

Patohia te paatene ASSIGN USERS ka whakakiia nga mara.

  1. Patohia te Kaiwhakamahi taka-iho ka kowhiri i tetahi tohu (hei tauira, imeera).
  2. Whakauruhia he uara ki te mara patea (hei tauira, te wahitau imeera).
  3. Mēnā kei te tūāpapa te rekoata kaiwhakamahi, ka tohu-aunoa te mara i te ingoa.
  4. Tīpakohia te kaiwhakamahi tika.
  5. Tīpakohia he tūranga kaiwhakamahi (kiritaki, taunoa, kaimahi ranei).
  6. Ka taea, tirohia te pouaka kia tukuna he panui imeera ki te kaiwhakamahi.
  7. Patohia te TIaki.

Ka taapirihia e Case Active te kaiwhakamahi ki te keehi. Inaianei ka taea e nga kaiwhakahaere te paatene Tiaki ki te kati i te matapihi, ki te taapiri i tetahi kaiwhakamahi hou, ki te tango kaiwhakamahi ranei.

Te taapiri i tetahi kaiwhakamahi hou

Ki te kore e kitea e te papa he rekoata o naianei, paatohia te paatene hei taapiri i tetahi kaiwhakamahi hou.

img

Whakakiia nga mara ka paato i te WHAKATAKI hei taapiri i te kaiwhakamahi ki te papaaho. I muri mai, whakaritea he mahi a te kaiwhakamahi ka paato ano i te WHAKATAKI kia tohua te kaiwhakamahi ki te keehi. Ka mutu, pawhiria te WHAKATAKI kia kotahi ano te wa hei tiaki i te keehi.
